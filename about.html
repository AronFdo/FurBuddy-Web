FurBuddy started with a simple problem: we kept forgetting our dog Buddy's vaccine dates. Sticky notes on the fridge didn't work. Spreadsheets felt cold. We wanted something that felt as warm as our relationship with our pets—and that actually reminded us when it mattered.

So we built an app. First for ourselves, then for friends who had the same "wait, when was Luna's last booster?" moment. Before long, vet clinics were asking if their clients could use it. Today, thousands of pet parents use FurBuddy to keep their dogs, cats, rabbits, and more healthy and on schedule. We're still a small team of pet lovers who code, design, and support every feature with real care.

"Our mission is simple: give every pet parent one place to track vaccines, medications, and health history—so you spend less time worrying and more time with your best friend."
